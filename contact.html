Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What's the best way to contact PrimeRealty for immediate assistance?

+

For immediate assistance, we recommend calling our main office at (555) 123-4567 during business hours (Mon-Fri 8AM-7PM, Sat 9AM-5PM). Our team is available to answer your questions and connect you with the right specialist. For non-urgent matters, you can email info@primerealty.com or use our contact forms above.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

+

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Phone calls are answered immediately during business hours. Email inquiries typically receive a response within 4-6 hours. For valuation requests, our experts will contact you within 48 hours to schedule an assessment. Appointment requests are confirmed within 2 hours of submission.

Do I need to schedule an appointment to visit your office?

+

While appointments are recommended to ensure you receive dedicated attention from our specialists, walk-ins are welcome during business hours. We recommend calling ahead if you need to meet with a specific agent or require specialized services. For complex matters like investment consultations or legal discussions, appointments are required to ensure adequate preparation time.

What information should I prepare before contacting you about selling my property?

+

To help us provide the best assistance, please have the following information ready: property address, square footage, number of bedrooms/bathrooms, recent renovations or upgrades, approximate age of the property, current mortgage information (if any), and any special features. If available, recent property photos and tax assessment documents can also be helpful for initial consultations.
