Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leadership

Two Decades of Excellence

Founded in 2025 by real estate visionary Charles Maina, PrimeRealty began as a small boutique firm with a big vision: to transform the real estate experience through transparency, expertise, and client-focused service.

What started as a three-person operation has grown into a full-service real estate firm with over 150 dedicated professionals across 8 regional offices. Our growth has been fueled by our commitment to delivering measurable results and building lasting relationships.

Today, we're recognized as industry leaders in property valuation, commercial real estate, and strategic investment consulting, but we've never lost sight of our founding principles.
