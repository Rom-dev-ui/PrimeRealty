Valuation FAQs

Get answers to common questions about property valuation

How accurate is your free property valuation?

+

Our free valuations are typically within 5-10% of the actual market value. For a precise valuation, we recommend scheduling an in-person appraisal with one of our certified appraisers. The free valuation provides an excellent starting point based on current market data, comparable properties, and your property's features.

Is there any obligation after receiving the free valuation?

+

Absolutely not. Our free valuation service comes with zero obligation. You're free to use the information however you wish, whether you decide to sell, refinance, or simply want to know your property's current market value. We won't pressure you to list with us, though we'd be happy to discuss your options if you're interested.

How long does it take to receive my valuation report?

+

After submitting your request, our valuation expert will contact you within 24 hours for a brief consultation. The comprehensive valuation report is typically delivered within 48 hours of that conversation. For complex properties or commercial valuations, it may take up to 72 hours to ensure maximum accuracy.

What information do you need for an accurate valuation?

+

For the most accurate valuation, we need: property address, type, size (square footage), number of bedrooms/bathrooms, property condition, special features (pool, garage, renovations), and recent improvements. The more details you provide, the more accurate our valuation will be. Don't worry if you're missing some information - our expert will help fill in the gaps during your consultation.
