Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does a property valuation typically take?

+

Standard residential property valuations are typically completed within 48-72 hours from the site inspection. Commercial properties or complex valuations may take 5-7 business days depending on the property type and scope of work required.

What are your service fees and how are they structured?

+

Our fees vary based on the service type and property value. Brokerage services typically work on commission (5-6% for residential, negotiable for commercial). Valuation services are typically fixed fees starting at $500. Development and investment services are customized based on project scope. All fees are transparently discussed and agreed upon before engagement.

Do you serve clients outside of your primary location?

+

Yes, we serve clients nationally and internationally. While our primary offices are in major metropolitan areas, we have a network of trusted partners and can provide remote advisory services. For on-site requirements like property inspections, we can either travel to the location or coordinate with local professionals in our network.

What qualifications do your consultants hold?

+

Our team includes certified appraisers (MAI, SRA), licensed real estate brokers, development professionals with architecture/engineering backgrounds, and investment analysts with CFA certifications. All team members undergo continuous professional development and maintain required state licenses and certifications.
